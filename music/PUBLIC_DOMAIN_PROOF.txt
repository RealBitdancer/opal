Provenance for the HSC tracks bundled in this folder (music/*.hsc).

The tracks come from HSCDEMO3.EXE, a 1992 AdLib music demo by Hannes Seifert
(author of the HSC format and the HSC AdLib Composer). Its title screen states,
verbatim:
----------------------------------------------------------------
AdLib Music Demo Release III. By Hannes Seifert 1992. Public Domain Version.
----------------------------------------------------------------

The "Public Domain Version" line covers the demo and Seifert's own tracks. It does
NOT cover every track: the demo bundles 38 songs, and its on-screen list gives a
copyright for each one. Many are game soundtracks Seifert composed under contract
and are marked with a publisher's copyright. Only the tracks the demo itself labels
"independent" or "public domain" are included in this folder; the copyrighted ones
are excluded.

Corroborating record
--------------------
The Internet Archive catalogs the original HSCDEMO3.EXE under Creative Commons
Public Domain Mark 1.0:
  Title:   Adlib Music Demo III
  Creator: Hannes Seifert
  Date:    1992
  License: http://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/mark/1.0/
  Source:  https://archive.org/details/HSCDEMO3.EXE
The archive description notes the demo "includes some of his compositions for the
games Osiris, Whale's Voyage, 1869, as well as some previously unused tracks" -
consistent with the per-track copyright labels above.
